About Fresh Fruita Vegeta

Fresh Fruita Vegeta started its business in 2017 in Lahore intending to reduce the gap in the Agri value chain by connecting farmers and end consumers, making sure that farmers get a fair price and our customers, top quality produce. We initially started online with a website and helpline for all order placements. In 2018 and 2019, we launched the android and iOS app respectively. We have maintained a standard for delivery of fresh fruits and vegetables, straight from the farm, to your doorstep. Our focus is on catering to the comfort and ease of our customers and ensuring they enjoy fresh fruits and vegetables. At Farm to Home, from the sourcing of the product, its purchasing, packaging and delivery are all done in house. We supervise your order from start to finish, making quality checks and thorough inspections before we deliver with caution and experience to our customers. Farm to Home not only operates online but also has a shop in Sector CCA FF Market Phase 4, D.H.A Lahore, so that you may come to visit us and handpick the fruits and vegetables you desire. Our team is passionate and committed to our cause, that we can help farmers, and at the same time bring their farm-fresh goodness to our customer's kitchen.

Our Values:

Living in Pakistan, where a large percentage of our labour force works in the agriculture sector, we believe it is our job to contribute to the community by making farming a profitable business. We play a role in the Agri value chain by connecting farmers to end-users and giving them a fair price for their produce in return. We buy products directly from the farmers and deliver to our customers. We believe it is our job to inform and promote the importance of consuming healthy and fresh produce and make it easily accessible to our customers. Enabling our customers to consume fresh fruits and vegetables that too at reasonable rates, without even having to step out of their house. The comfort, convenience and health of our customers is our top priority and we promise to always provide them with fresh, top quality products while at the same time making a difference in the agriculture community of Pakistan.

Our Business Model:

B2B:

We also cater to businesses and restaurants. Resultants, shops and banquet halls that require fruits and vegetables in large quantities; we deliver them their products in a timely and efficient manner without comprising on the freshness and quality of the fruits and vegetables.

Our Services:

We are currently operating online in Lahore and have a shop in Sector CCA FF Phase 4, D.H.A Lahore. You can visit our shop and handpick the fruits and vegetables of your liking. Or you can also relax at home and place your order online through our user-friendly website or application and let us take care of the rest.
